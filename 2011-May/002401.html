Search the eLua-dev mailing list for "PIC32" and you'll find some threads
about the eLua PIC32 porting efforts. Basically it's going very slow because
of some problems with Microchip's less than ideal (to put it lightly)
licensing of their tools/libraries which tends to piss open source people
off (and nobody can really blame them). That said, I believe we're getting
there. Just, you know, slowly.
