I have confirmed that the velocity capture is functioning.  That is all I
needed to test for my purposes.  I would have tested more if I had a real

http://ww1.microchip.com/downloads/en/devicedoc/91091A.pdf

FYI...The PWM on the LM3S8962 doesn't work at frequencies lower than around
6Hz.  TI forum said it was because of 16 bit PWM timer.

Also, I had to add #define BUILD_QEI to get it to work as I said earlier.
