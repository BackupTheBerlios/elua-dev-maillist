I'm in process of making the USB client stack more generic.  I was thinking of a directory structure:

Top level
elua/src/modules/USB
 /common

 /device
        /cdc-serial
/core

/host
       /core

Each platform would then have handlers for USB HW.  It would be good to make sure this works on a couple of platforms.


The main issue, is handling full speed and high speed.  Does the AVR32 have high speed usb?
