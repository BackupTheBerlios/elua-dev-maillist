There are quite a lot of AVR32 developers here on the list. The most recent
and complete AVR32 port was done by the
SimpleMachine<http://www.simplemachines.it/>guys (Nuccio, Martin,
Sergio, ...), who are designing a board specially for
eLua, the AVR32-based
Mizar32<http://www.simplemachines.it/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=13&Itemid=24>.

Renaud Cerrato also worked on a port for the EVK1101 (pretty similar to the
EVK1100). There are some more infos on these boards at
http://wiki.eluaproject.net/AtmelEVK110x
