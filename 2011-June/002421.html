On 6/3/2011 9:39 AM, Tim michals wrote:
> I'm in process of making the USB client stack more generic.  I was 
> thinking of a directory structure:
> Top level
> elua/src/modules/USB
>  /common
>  /device
> /cdc-serial
> /core
> /host
> /core
>
> Each platform would then have handlers for USB HW.  It would be good 
> to make sure this works on a couple of platforms.
>
> The main issue, is handling full speed and high speed.  Does the AVR32 
> have high speed usb?
Some models (e.g. AT32UC3Axxx ) do; they're still one of the few MCUs 
with high speed USB 2.0 in quad flatpack.  Since HS USB is slowing 
becoming more common (e.g. the new STM32F2 series has it), I'd say it's 
good to plan for it.
