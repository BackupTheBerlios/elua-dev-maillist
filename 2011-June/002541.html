> Hello all, I'm new to the eLua group.  I was curious to see if anyone has

We've been using XBee modules for sometime now, with good results and using
the regular uart module to talk to them.
It would be nice to have a more specialized/high level module to threat them
though and this is the reason why someone started the entry on the Wiki (I
guess).
I don't know if there are other Zigbee or XBee modules out there with SPI,
I2C or even CAN interfaces. Our experience (@Led Lab, PUC-Rio) is only with
the serial versions.
This seems to be something that could even be implemented in Lua, if the
space requirements for the final uses are not too tight.
Ives Negreiros, Teo Benjamin and maybe some other collaborators can possibly
add something to this.
