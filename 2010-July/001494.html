Hello,

Today we (Me, Dado and Francisco santanna) ran for the first time a reactive
language (http://www.lua.inf.puc-rio.br/luagravity/) in an embedded system!
Reactive languages are pretty cool to solving problems of programs that have
many states (state machines) and for the management of interruptions (not
for eLua, yet), so I think that the result we had today is a good step first
step =)
More news to come.
