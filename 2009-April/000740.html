Hi,

We will have such a FS at some point (I have a proof of concept ready), but
I can't tell you why, as right now I'm so busy that I don't even get to work
on the 0.6 docs anymore (hope this will change soon).
I do not recommend using FAT on Flash directly. You might kill your Flash
soon. FAT was not designed to work directly with a medium such as Flash that
generally needs a wear leveling algorithm for a nice, long life :)

Best,
Bogdan
