> P.S. What is the best platform to buy that has Ethernet and CAN? Thanks.
>

This can depend on many other aspects of your applications but currently:

- The EK-LM3S8962 (Luminary Micro/Texas Instruments Cortex-M3, 50MHz, 64KB
RAM) runs eLua very well and has both Ethernet and CAN ready to used.

- The STR-E912 (Olimex ARM9, 96MHz, 96KB RAM {it has some more but not
contiguous}, external ram capable) is a bit more expensive but really rocks,
although our net module is not yet available for it.

We've started a page on the eLua User
Labs<http://wiki.eluaproject.net/Boards>a while ago, to help users
answer this question according to their needs,
budgets, feelings, .... and any help on the wiki is welcome too.
