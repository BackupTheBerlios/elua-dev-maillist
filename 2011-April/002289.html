On Fri, Apr 22, 2011 at 12:01 PM, gutaku <gutaku at gmail.com> wrote:

> I'm working with the Gaisler Research GR-RASTA-106-SVGA board. Between all
> the components, it contains a LEON2 SPARC V8 Processor with FPU, 4MB SRAM,
> 128MB SDRAM and 8MB Flash PROM.
>

You have quite a bit of resources :)


> Which would be the different between porting Lua to my platform, or porting
> eLua to my platform?
>

eLua is Lua + low resources optimizations + lots of peripheral libraries +
other goodies. It does insist on being the "system controller" at the
moment, as it handlse very low-level stuff like board bringup, interrupts
and memory management. If that's not OK you might want to stick with stock
Lua.
