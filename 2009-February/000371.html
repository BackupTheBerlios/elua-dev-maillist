Hello Guys,
   BGA is also a barrier downhere in Brazil, although there are now some
companies that assemble them fast and not that more expensive (= not more
then the usual overhead for non-huge quantities).
   My feeling is that, if a board/stamp will be targeted at hobbysts,
independent developers and/or small companies, BGA is not very welcome.
   On the format, DIP or SIP can be very hard too, with some fancy chips
full of internal toys and features (228 pins on some NXPs !!!!!).

Best
Dado
