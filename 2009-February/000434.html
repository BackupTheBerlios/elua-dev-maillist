> Can you tell us what is the code-size limit for IAR EWARM free version ?

The free version for ARM is limited to 32K of code. The two commercial
versions, one limited to 256K of code and the other one unlimited. The 256K
version is around $3K.

I have used EWARM to debug ISRs with Luminary MCUs. It has worked well.
