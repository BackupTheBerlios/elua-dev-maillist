There is always a mix of accesses to an external SPI ram
(fram/mram/sram) and to internal ram and internal flash. So 2us
int32 access to an external SPI ram does not mean the whole system
will run @ 500kHz :) 

I do not know how it is with eLUA, but I guess when accessing
external SPI ram 20-30% of time you may get  _much_  better overall
average speed..
