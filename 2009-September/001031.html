MCU Days is a one day TI marketing seminar.  It's an overview of TI's MSP430
16-bit MCUs, Stellaris and OMAP L1 ARM MCUs, and F2000 DSPs.  One good
reason for going: discounts on a selection of development tools.

The MCU Days page is at
http://focus.ti.com/mcu/docs/mcuorphan.tsp?contentId=35970
The MCU Days Brazil page is at
http://www.ti.com/ww/br/prod_mcu_mcuday2009.html

There are also seminars in the US and Europe.  I was too busy to go; I did
attend a probably similar STR9 seminar a couple years ago.  It was
worthwhile.  I heard some good questions, ST raffled off a couple of dev
kits, and had a coupon for 50% ($100 off a $200 dev kit) for the rest of us.
