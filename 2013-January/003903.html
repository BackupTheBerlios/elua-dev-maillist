The LPC1768 is the MCU used on the MBED boards, but I don't know if the
GPIO pins of the chip are mapped too or only the board pins are.

If they are all mapped you should have no problem running eLua on that
board, but you might have to change the PLL settings if the crystal
frequency is different from the MBED.
