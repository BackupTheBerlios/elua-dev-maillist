......... and I might also suggest you start flashing a special built image
for the EK-LM3S8962 we have prepared and made available on the site under
the "Official Custom Build Binaries", the LM3S Games.

http://elua.giga.puc-rio.br/downloads/elua_games_EK-LM3S8962.bin

You will have an autorun menu show up on your OLED display, where you can
select among 6~7 diferent games and demos for the LM3S board and have some
fun even before typing your first command on an eLua Terminal.
