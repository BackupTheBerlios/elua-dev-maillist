Hello list,
   Jesus, I will contact you in pvt but I would like to publicly thank you
and Micromint <http://www.micromint.com/> very much for this and for the
support  you've been showing to eLua.
   For those who don't know yet, the SD/MMC Cards FAT file system support
for eLua was a Jesus Alvarez contribution, as stated in our Credits page (
http://www.eluaproject.net/en_comunity.html#credits)
   Micromint also mentions eLua in the Software section of the Eagle 50 page
at http://www.micromint.com/index.php/SBC/eagle-50.html

   eLua is an independent project, none of us nor the project have any
official support and we are very thankful for these references and glad to
see eLua is beeing useful for many different platforms and uses.
   Here in my Lab @PUC-Rio, me and even the students struggle continuously
to buy our own kits and to get funds from other projects to spread eLua use
among other groups, departments and researchers. This is also true for
Bogdan in Bucharest. Specifically, here we are very close to be able to buy
a couple of (our firsts) nice Eagle50 boards but I will try to adapt some
other demos for it, in time for ESC Silicon Valley.

   Thank you very much again for you code, help and support.

Best
Dado
