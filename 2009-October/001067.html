I named it initial since I excluded some of the hacks for ancient  
versions of IE, and I haven't tested to see how far back it will work  
without them.

I wanted to have a browser that needed such things in front of me to  
ensure that those would render correctly before committing them (since  
some are fudges associated with positioning/sizing)

At the moment though, it should do the HTML indentation pretty well in  
the code generator and it works with modern W3C-compliant browsers :-)
