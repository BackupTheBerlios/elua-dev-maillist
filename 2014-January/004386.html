Yes, very nice x2 !

For the first elua load, use the mikroe's bootloader:
http://www.mikroe.com/downloads/get/1944/mini_m4_mikrobootloader_v210b.zip

or erase all memory with sthe ST-LINK V2, and load elua (with remove 
before the protect by fuse).

Pins ST-LINK V2 ___ Pins STM32

1 or 2    ___    3,3V
7  ____  PA13
9  ____   PA14
13   ____   PB3 (optional)
15   ____   /reset
4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, 18, 20 ___ GND

Best'

Albert
