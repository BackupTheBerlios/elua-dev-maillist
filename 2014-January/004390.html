Currently, eLua's I2C library (and platform interface) is lacking in many
ways (mostly because my lack of knowledge regarding various implementations
of I2C interfaces from various manufacturers at the time I've designed
that) and probably needs a complete redesign. I understand the need for
alternate solutions. Since I2C is a low speed interface, a bit-banged Lua
implementation is suitable in this case. Not so much with SPI, because that
works at much higher speeds.
