Hello there,
   As most of you must know from the Lua list, the videos of the Lua
Workshop were made available and I'm posting here the links compilation.
   As it was basically a portuguese-spoken event this year, most talks were
given in portuguese but some (or some parts) were in english too and I noted
below on the list.
   Bogdan and I did a presentation on eLua.
   Students from my Lab (Téo Benjamin and Ives Negreiros) did another one
the eLua Games they have created and some other projects using eLua in
PUC-Rio.
   The Led Lab web dev team (Rafael, Vagner, Guilherme and Carlos Eduardo)
also presented other projects using Lua and Kepler.
   So here goes the list:
