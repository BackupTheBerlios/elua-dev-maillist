I included subtitles in English in my talk about LuaGravity in the Lua
Workshop'09.
http://thesynchronousblog.wordpress.com/lua-workshop09-video/

LuaGravity is a reactive language that implements the synchronous approach
for concurrency. It is roughly based on Esterel and FrTime, two synchronous
reactive languages, the former having an imperative style, the latter being
functional.
