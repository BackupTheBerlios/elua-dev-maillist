...I can officialy announce that eLua can now be compiled from Windows! I
used the CodeSourcery toolchain and it works just fine. Youl'll need python,
scons and a set of common shell utils (rm, mv and friends) compiled for
Windows (download this from
http://gnuwin32.sourceforge.net/packages/coreutils.htm for example),
everything in your $PATH, and everything else should happen just like in
Linux. A full tutorial will follow in doc/. Remember to update your latest
sources, cause the Windows build needs a SConstruct fix that I checked in
today. Yet another thing to add in 0.6 :)
