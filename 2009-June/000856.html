Hello There,
   As some of you may have heard on the Lua list already, the  Lua Workshop
 2009 will be held at PUC-Rio on October 6-7, 2009, just  before SBGames
2009 and SIBGRAPI 2009. The activities on October 7 will  be dedicated to
the use of Lua in games, as part of Lua Games 2009, a pre-event of SBGames
2009.
   eLua will be presented on the first day of the Workshop, hopefully with
the presence of Bogdan too (to be confirmed).
   Although it is more of a portuguese-language-oriented event, you are all
invited to come and to visit where Lua was born :) There will be talks
presented in english by foreing speakers as well of round tables but the
ones presented in portuguese will have no simultaneous translation. You can
always skip some talks and spend some time on the beach, less then a mile
from PUC :)
   Pré registration, more information and (soon) the final program can be
found at http://www.lua.org/wshop09.html#english. The event will be free as
all the previous Lua Workshops but the space is limited, so a registration
is required to guarantee you place.
   Please feel free to post any questions here too. You are all welcome and
we'll certainly talk a lot about eLua.

Greetings
Dado
