Doing a PCB with large fine pitch chips (e.g. QFP-208) is time consuming and
requires someone with experience.  Smaller chips with larger pads should be
a lot easier.  Search Sparkfun for stencil info -- there's a company that
makes low cost laser cut stencils (not professional quality, but good enough
for hobby use) but they don't really advertise that capability.
