Thinking about this, I don't think we should worry about getting all
of these under one particular YouTube account.  We might want to
aggregate them, say based on a common tag like "eluaproject" or
something similar, so that people can find videos about it, but given
the way these accounts work, it might be better just to allow people
to upload with whatever account and then link them in the wiki.
Perhaps Dado or someone else can have a curated list of videos that
only go on a main account, but we shouldn't stop putting stuff up just
because of this account issue.

Feel free to also use things like:
http://blip.tv/

That service is nice because you can set up distribution through other
various other sites from a single upload:
http://blip.tv/faq/#distribution
