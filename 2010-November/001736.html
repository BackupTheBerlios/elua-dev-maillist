Platform naming in eLua follows closely the CPU manufacturer's line of
products. An example: lpc24xx and lpc 288x. If you go to the NXP site,
you'll find a lot of CPUs under the LPC24xx family and a few (but more
than one) CPUs in the LPC288x family. Your platform should be named
following a similar reasoning. I'll take a look at the ColdFire
product tree and come up with a suggestion as soon as I have some time
for this.
