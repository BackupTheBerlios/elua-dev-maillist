LTR compatible wrapper generation for eLua has been accepted by the SWIG
community.
The patch was merged into main trunk. For now, the code can be obtained
through a GNU
tarball from the official SWIG SVN repository.

http://swig.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/swig/trunk/ 
(swig -lua -help)

LTR compatible SWIG can certainly be put to good use. The generated wrapper
was tested
on LM3S8962 and TRICORE.

Please give me your feedback.

Cheers!
