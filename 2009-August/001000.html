Ah, you're right, I forgot about that. So, there you go, you can have eLua
(just) under 64k (although I think that this is indeed mostly didactic, not
truly functional from an engineering point of view). In that case though,

resource-constrained micros in the future, but I can't foresee how low I can
go (sort of speaking :)).
