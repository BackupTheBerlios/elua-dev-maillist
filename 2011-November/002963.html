One other thing occurs to me based on having gotten one of the eval
kits where this was the case before:

Can you check with the luminary flash tool whether or not the USER0
and USER1 registers are programmed with the MAC address?  When I
originally got my kit these registers were all zeros and I couldn't
get it to do any networking prior to that point.

Given that I've seen this more than once I'm going to add a note to
add a feature to check for this case in the future and substitute a
MAC when it happens.
