I'm glad to be the catalyst for a PIC32 port; I do think the PIC32MX795F512H
looks pretty interesting.  I was very surprised that Microchip included so
much RAM, because in the past PIC micros have been pretty light on RAM.

It looks like the PIC32MX795F512's are just starting to reach the
distributors (e.g. Mouser USA has 10 in stock).

Another bit of news: Atmel is working on a single precision FPU for the
AVR32 U3 series.  Since no parts were announced, it'll be a while (my guess:
>2 years to reach distribution).  But a AVR32U3 series with >= 128K SRAM and
FPU would be pretty sweet.

--Tony
