Hi,

There isn't currently a port of eLua for the SAM7S128, and while it can be
done, it will certainly be quite limited because of the relatively low
resources of this MCU (128k Flash/32k RAM). For ARM MPUs, a recommended
"base" eLua configuration is 256k Flash/64k RAM.

Best,
Bogdan
