I do not have an Ethernet jack to connect as well, so I plugged a cable
directly, using 4 of the 8 wires inside it, to RD+, RD-, TX+ and TX-. I
grabbed an example from MBed's website and it's now running a small
webserver since yesterday here!

Now, port it to eLua would be a great good, I think. I do not know if I'm
right, but MBed now uses lwIp for tcp stack and ethernet drivers, so, I do
not know in what it implies.
