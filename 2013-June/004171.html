At the last Lua workshop (Nov 2012, Washington DC, US), I gave a
presentation on an in-memory (for now) database tool for microcontrollers.
It is written in pure Lua and targeted for use with eLua.

The presentation is in:

http://www.lua.org/wshop12/Freund.pdf

The video of the presentation is in:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Olc0fAuY4nU

This is to announce that a community version of picoDB is available in
Github at:

https://github.com/digysol/picoDB

This is the beta release.

This and future releases are available as open source under the Apache
license.

Welcome your feedback.
