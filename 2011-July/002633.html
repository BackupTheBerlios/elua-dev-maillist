Hello list,
   On this Friday July 8th, some eLua services will be offline for a few
hours, due to a hardware maintenance and a migration to a new machine.
   Services will be down from 17:00 (5 PM) to 20:00 (8 PM) GMT, with some
possibility of returning sooner than that.
   Services that will be affected are:

- The eLua Wiki (wiki.eluaproject.net)
- The eLua Web Builder (builder.eluaproject.net)
- The eLua Tracker (tracker.eluaproject.net)

   The eLua main web site (www.eluaproject.net) and the experimental new
version of the site (new.eluaproject.net) will not be affected.
   This should fix the issues we've had along the last weeks due to the
server hanging up for no reason.
   The new machine was made available by Raul Nunes and PUC-Rio (thanks
!!!), until we find a way to fund and choose a professional option to host
our services.
   We apologise for the inconvenience and thank you for the comprehension.
