And that is exactly what I was telling you. The interface is called 1-Wire,
check the wikipedia link from my previous e-mail. Some MCUs have 1-Wire
implemented in hardware, but only very few of them, so your best shot is to
emulate it with a GPIO pin. I did this once and from what I remember it was
quite an easy job.
