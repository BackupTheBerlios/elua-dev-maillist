> AFAIK sockets are not reuse-able in this way.
> A call to accept in a BSD socket architecture will always create a new
> socket, see: http://linux.die.net/man/2/accept
>
> Keep-alive is a completely different concept: the same connection is kept
> alive for additional requests to the server.
