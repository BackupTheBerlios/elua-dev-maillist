FYI, for SAM7/ other platform port'ers and perhaps for build/ porting doc,
faq purpose...

In case you're planning to use DBGU for serial shell/ terminal/ console;
please keep in mind... 
1) Choosing CON_UART_ID in platform_conf.h won't help. Instead make sure to
replace AT91C_BASE_USx with 
    AT91C_BASE_DBGU
2) Either explicitly enable receive interrupt for DBGU or simply
disable(comment out) both the RX buffering defines 

This allowed proper shell operation on my SAM7 module.
