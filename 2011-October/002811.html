Well, I do have an old datasheet called: AN3156 USB DFU protocol used in the
STM32™ bootloader, however it doesn't detail binary files, only DFU commands
per se.

I could write something in C++, but unfortunately I have no way to test
stuff, since the only DFU chip I have is an ATmega32u2. My STM32 does not
have a DFU bootloader (only the connectivity line has it). Maybe I can try
it with the maple DFU bootloader. By the way, if the maple is programmed
with DFU, and they have an Arduino-based IDE, maybe they already have those
converters.

More info: http://leaflabs.com/docs/bootloader.html

Ronan
