Eureka, or almost.

The stlink project doesn't write the flash.  The dfu-util project doesn't
talk to the stm32, and I temporarily bricked my board when I tried hacking
the protocol.

But this project,
http://gitorious.org/~tormod/unofficial-clones/dfuse-dfu-util, just wrote
the demonstration binary back onto the board, so my ex-brick is now flashing
its leds again.  And it's willing to flash raw .bin files, too, as well as
the ST v1.1a dfu suffixed files.

My image doesn't work when flashed, but that will be easier to fix with a
way to flash the image.
