I agree with you on this... but do ensure that the chosen service
allows you to make a usable backup of your data, and that you can
recover your site from that backup. Yes, it's less work to maintain,
is probably more reliable day-to-day and costs less, but they can
withdraw the service with no notice. Even being an enormous
world-class site with many thousands of users doesn't seem to make a
difference to this (e.g. yahoo geocities).
 Keeping this essential freedom is also necessary, in case you need to
change service provider or decide to host it in-house again for some
reason.
