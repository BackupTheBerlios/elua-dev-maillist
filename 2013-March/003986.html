> Hello guys,

That's only partially correct. uIP didn't quite evolve to lwIP, they are
different stacks with different capabilities and quite different memory
requirements (which is why eLua uses uIP at the moment).

> iwIP since it has more capabilities than uIP, that could extend featuresin

>

This is something we take under consideration, but no final decision was
made yet.


>

By all means, please do it. While you're at it, you can also suggest ways
to improve our platform interface and overall network API, since that's an
area where eLua needs a lot of improvement.
