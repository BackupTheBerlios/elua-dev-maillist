> ..this is about the availability and the price of the SRAMS. BGA
> packages are nogo for DIY, SDRAMS are nogo for stm32f4, and PSRAMS
> are slow (~70ns) and BGA.
> The only 10ns TSOP candidates I see are:
> IS61WV102416BLL-10TLI (10ns 1Mx16)
> IS61WV20488BLL-10TLI (10ns, 2Mx8)
> The big Q is where to get them cheap?? P.
>
> Nowhere....they're ~24USD on Digikey, and smaller sizes cost about the
same per bit (4Mb is ~$6, 8Mb is ~$18).  I did a Digikey search, and nobody
else (IDT, Cypress, etc) looks significantly cheaper.  If you need speed
but want to spend less, look at using a smaller size, although ISSI's
smaller SRAMs are in different packages (e.g. 44-TSOP instead of 48-TSOP).
(Side note: huge internal SRAM is coming at a hopefully affordable price:
Freescale's next generation Kinetis ARM MCU's will have 512K SRAM).
