Hi Richard,
You can use the bit module to do the usual bit operations (or, and, xor,
...):
http://bitop.luajit.org/api.html

To assemble multiple bytes, you could do
a = 3
b = 5
c = a..b
and have c = "35"
or spi.write(0, a, b)
