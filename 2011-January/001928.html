Hello list,
   We've added some infos and a video of Moonlight to the eLua User Labs
wiki at http://wiki.eluaproject.net/moonlight.
   Moonlight is a iMCU / Circuit Cellar contest 4th place winner project by
Bogdan Marinescu.
   Multi-functional Led Panels powered by eLua.
   The project's source code, docs and media can be found at
https://github.com/elua/moonlight.
   The name Moonlight is an allusion to "the moon", which is the English
meaning of Lua.

Enjoy
Dado
