Hello,
I'd like to give eLua a shot. Would anybody be able to give me some
directions on getting eLua on a Netduino, Netduino plus or Arduino DUE. I
suppose what I'm looking for is a board that is widely available and is the
most easily flash-able.

My goal is to have fun and investigate what can be done with eLua as well
as investigate things like how to port eLua to new platforms.

I have read the wiki on which boards are supported. I have also read some
of the tutorials. I did not see the kind of step by step instructions for
flashing eLua to the Netduino that were available for some of the other
boards.

I'd like to get my feet wet in the most user recommended fashion and help
out with any porting projects if I am able.

 In the long term I would like to help produce tutorials on how to use eLua
to do small experimental projects. Basically the same thing as what can be
found in the user projects, but I think it would be nice to have a
whole series that go with a specific device. That way if somebody new
bought a device they could choose one with lots of supporting lessons or
other material.

Full disclosure: I may get 1 or 2 credits for independent study time from
school.
